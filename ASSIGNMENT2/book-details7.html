Good Girl, Bad Blood

Author: Holly Jackson

Price: $19.99

"The crime-thriller sequel to the no.1 debut of 2019, A GOOD GIRL'S GUIDE TO MURDER, is a New York Times bestseller and brilliantly crafted. A GOOD GIRL'S GUIDE TO MURDER was the winner of the British Book Awards Children's Book of the Year 2020 and shortlisted for the Waterstones Children's Book Prize 2020. This nail-biting, taut, and pacy thriller has earned high praise from The Guardian, describing Jackson as a homegrown thriller writer to watch. Pip Fitz-Amobi is no longer a detective. Together with Ravi Singh, she released a true-crime podcast about the murder case they solved last year, which went viral. However, when someone she knows goes missing, Pip breaks her promise to stay away from investigations and starts looking for Jamie Reynolds, who the police refuse to search for. Her search for Jamie uncovers more dark secrets about her town and everyone is listening. Will Pip find Jamie before it's too late? This book is perfect for fans of One of Us Is Lying, Eva Dolan, C L Taylor, We Were Liars and Riverdale. A Good Girl's Guide to Murder has received high praise from various sources. The Guardian describes it as a "taut, compulsively readable, elegantly plotted thriller," while Laura Purcell, bestselling author of The Silent Companions, calls it a "fiendishly-plotted mystery that kept me guessing until the very end." Peter James, an award-winning and no.1 bestselling crime thriller author of the Detective Superintendent Roy Grace series, commends the book for its fine characterization and great energy to the writing. Savannah Brown, the author of The Truth About Keeping Secrets, describes the book as "twisty, compulsive and so, so clever," while Yasmin Rahman, author of All the Things We Never Said, calls the ending "pure genius." Fiona Noble, The Bookseller, describes the book as "twisty and compelling," while Aisha Busby, author of A Pocketful of Stars, praises the book for its delight from start to finish."
